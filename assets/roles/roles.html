Brainstorm the client/brand requirements with the account management team or the client representatives and translate the same into a creative brief

Develop the creative strategy, the creative concept and the key messages using the creative brief as the roadmap

Brainstorm the creative concepts with the Head of Art, develop the appropriate imagery for the concepts and decide on the ATL/BTL collaterals to carry the concept

De-brief the creative team on the creative brief and the creative concepts with the aid of mood boards and doodles/sketches

Guide the creative team as and when they develop the doodles/sketches into computerized artworks and layouts; oversee the execution process and refine the artworks, taking into account the feedback of the account management, till they are deemed fit for presentation

Ensure that the artworks developed are in line with each and every brand’s pre- determined graphic guidelines

Develop the copy (headlines, sub head, body copy) for all communication collaterals in line with the creative brief and key messages

Develop the content for print materials like brochures, annual reports, flyers etc. and publishing materials like press releases, speeches etc.

Proofread all the communication collaterals before going to client and prior to final production and ensure they are devoid of any typo mistakes

Proofread the figures in financial statement ads against originals provided by the client’s external auditors

Key member of the account management team that serviced the major clients of the agency

Compiling the creative presentations including developing the overall content, creative strategy and the rationales that should accompany the concept and artwork presentation to clients

Accompany the account management team for major client presentations and briefing sessions

Work closely with the account management team in developing communication plans, media strategies and media mix for the creative solutions developed by the agency

Liaise directly with the client representatives and the account management team for the day-to-day operations of the agency’s accounts

Reporting directly to the General Manager of the agency

Act as the single window interface between the creative team and other departments of the agency

Develop daily project schedules for the creative team in line with pre-determined client deadlines and ensure that deadlines are strictly adhered to

Evaluate the performance of the creative team and recommend performance-based financial incentives and increments

Responsible for the quality control of all the solutions developed by the creative team

In charge of maintaining the overall infrastructure of the creative department like purchasing machines, upgrading software programs, sourcing creative resources like image libraries etc.

Responsible for the HR needs of the creative team
